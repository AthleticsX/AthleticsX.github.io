FAQs

How does AthleticsX work?

AthleticsX uses AI and Computer Vision to analyze player performance based on uploaded match videos. Coaches and athletes can access detailed insights and statistics for performance improvement and evaluation.

What sports are currently supported?

AthleticsX is currently focused on Tennis but plans to expand to other sports soon. Stay tuned for updates on new sports additions!

How can coaches and athletes communicate?

AthleticsX is aligned with the NCAA's recruiting cycle. Coaches and athletes can communicate through AthleticsX when permitted. 

Is AthleticsX in the development phase?

Yes, AthleticsX is continuously evolving to provide cutting-edge solutions for sports recruitment and performance analysis.

Can I access previous player performances?

Absolutely! Coaches and athletes can access both recent and previous player performances to track progress and identify areas for improvement.
